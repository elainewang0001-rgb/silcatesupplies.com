About Us

"Unity for Progress, Wisdom for New Horizons"

We are a technology-based enterprise focusing on inorganic silicon compounds, recognized as a national-level "Little Giant" enterprise and a national high-tech enterprise. We serve as the vice-chairman unit of the China Inorganic Silicon Compounds Association.

With an annual production capacity of 100,000 tons, We operate a digital intelligent manufacturing base and has built 15 provincial and municipal scientific and technological innovation platforms, including the Provincial Academician Workstation and Provincial Postdoctoral Innovation and Practice Base.

The company has partnered with top universities including Jilin University and Dalian University of Technology through joint R&D centers, translating laboratory breakthroughs into core market competitiveness.
